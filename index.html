Consulting For Capital

Pan-European Commercial Advisory Alliance

Consulting For Capital is an exclusive international consulting partnership between three of Europe’s leading commercial advisory firms. It has been created to support ambitious, high-growth corporations and private equity firms in resolving their most critical commercial challenges across strategy, mergers & acquisitions and business transformation.

Strategic Alliance

Consulting For Capital is a partnership built on a belief in the power of cross-border, sector-focused teams, entrepreneurial spirit and investment in top-tier talent to produce outstanding results for our clients and employees.

The alliance was formed to solve the most important and complex challenges for owners, investors, and managers of market-leading businesses in Europe, and beyond.

Our mission is to be the leading adviser to private investors and high-growth corporations in Europe.

With 170+ consultants in key economic hubs across London, Paris, Madrid, Munich, Frankfurt and Zurich, Consulting For Capital combines the deep local knowledge, craftsmanship and high-touch service levels of three entrepreneurial firms into a pan-European consulting platform with scale, reach and the collective experience of over 1,700 client projects.

Services

Get in Touch

We support investors and management teams across the entire investment cycle. Our work clusters into three categories — Investment, Value Creation and Sale. In each, we blend evidence-based analysis with pragmatic, hands-on delivery using joint CFC teams across countries. Typical outputs include investment theses & commercial DD, value-creation plans with pricing and go-to-market playbooks, and vendor materials including equity story, business plan and IM input.
